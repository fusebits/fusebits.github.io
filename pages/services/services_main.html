Electronics Design

We can design a boards for a wide variety of applications and technologies. With equipment in house for prototype development, every design we make gets fully tested before they get to you. The designs can be optimized for large scale manufacture, or just prototypes for cost effectiveness.

Here are some of the types of projects we do:

  • Arduino Compatible Boards
  • USB
  • Wireless/RF (Bluetooth, Wifi, NRF, HAM radio )
  • IOT/Sensor systems
  • Audio Devices
  • Power Electronics
  • Robotics
  • Video Game/Computer interfaces

We generally will do up to 4 layer board designs, but will do more if necessary.

Manufacturing

Want to make a few one off prototypes or produce thousands of units? We can help. We have on-site equipment for testing and producing electronics at prototype quantities. For larger quantities we can help you set up your entire supply and manufacturing chain, and optimize your design for manufacture.
